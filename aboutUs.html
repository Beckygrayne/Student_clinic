ABOUT KTECH CLINIC


Welcome to knust Student Clinic!! We are dedicated to providing excellent healthcare services to students and the academic community. Our team of skilled medical professionals and staff work together to ensure your well-being and support your academic success.

We offer medical services to students and staff, including general check-ups, vaccinations, health screenings, mental health support, and more. Our goal is to create a safe and welcoming environment where you can receive the care you need.

Our student clinic is conveniently located on-campus, making it easy for you to access medical care without disrupting your studies. Whether you have a minor ailment or need ongoing medical support, we are here to assist you.

We prioritize your privacy and confidentiality, and our staff follows the highest standards of medical ethics and professionalism. You can trust us with your health and well-being.

If you have any questions or want to schedule an appointment, feel free to reach out to us. We look forward to serving you and being a part of your journey towards a healthy and successful academic life.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to be a leading student clinic known for excellence in healthcare services and a commitment to student welfare. We strive to continuously improve and expand our services to meet the evolving needs of the academic community.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare services that promote the well-being and academic success of students. We aim to create a caring and supportive environment where students can receive the care they need to thrive both academically and personally.
