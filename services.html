STUDENT CLINIC SERVICES

VACCINATION

Vaccination: is a vital preventive measure to protect you from various diseases. Especially as Covid-19 is concerned Our clinic provides a comprehensive range of vaccinations, including routine vaccinations and specific vaccines for travel purposes. By getting vaccinated, you can build immunity and safeguard yourself from infections.

Benefits:

  • Prevents the spread of infectious diseases
  • Ensures your health and well-being
  • Complies with vaccination requirements for travel and academic purposes

DENTAL CARE

Our dental care service aims to promote oral health and hygiene among students. Regular dental check-ups and treatments are crucial for preventing dental issues and maintaining a healthy smile. Our qualified dentists provide a range of dental services, including cleanings, check-ups, and minor treatments.

Benefits:

  • Maintains healthy teeth and gums
  • Prevents dental problems like cavities and gum disease
  • Enhances overall oral hygiene and appearance

EYE CARE

Eye health is essential for academic success, and our eye care service aims to address common eye conditions and provide vision care. Our team of optometrists offers comprehensive eye examinations, prescription glasses, and contact lens fittings.

Benefits:

  • Improves vision and eye comfort
  • Detects and manages eye conditions
  • Enhances academic performance

MENTAL HEALTH

We understand the importance of mental well-being in your academic journey. Our mental health services are here to support you through any challenges you may face. Our professional counselors provide confidential counseling sessions to help you cope with stress, anxiety, and other mental health concerns.

Benefits:

  • Promotes emotional well-being
  • Provides coping strategies and support
  • Fosters a positive and healthy mindset

Please note: For serious eye health issues, we recommend visiting the main clinic (Knust Main Clinic) for specialized care.

For urgent medical concerns outside of our regular hours, please contact the main clinic or seek medical attention at the nearest healthcare facility.
